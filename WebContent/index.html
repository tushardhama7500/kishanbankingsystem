Welcome to KCC Bank

The principal objective of the KCC Bank is to achieve and maintain price stability. To attain this goal, the Bank performs a number of functions in accordance with the modern central banking practices. An important function of the Bank is to formulate and execute monetary policy for Lesotho. Other functions include issuing of currency, serving as a banker for the Lesotho Government and the commercial banks, acting as a custodian of the countryâ€™s foreign reserves and supervision of financial institutions. It also acts as an advisor to the government on financial issues specifically, and more generally on macro-economic issues.
